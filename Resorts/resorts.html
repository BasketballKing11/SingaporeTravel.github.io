Resorts

Where to Stay?

Singapore is host to a number of resorts ranging from practical and comfortable, to five-star luxary. We have listed below our reccomended hotels with each one providing a description, price, and link to the hotel's website, allowing you to make the decision that best fits your needs.

*These hotels are included in travel bundles which might result in lower prices*

Marina Bay Sands Resort

The Marina Bay Sands Resort was opened in 2010. It reflects the highest quality of hotel standards here in Singapore. With a stay here you'll find dozens of shops, a museum, large theatre, multiple five-star restaurants, an art exhibit, and an infinity pool running the entire length of the roof of this fifty-five story marvel.

The Warehouse Hotel

The Warehouse Hotel is the perfect mix between luxury and affordablity. Constructed in 1895, the historic building now sits in the heart of Singapore. Overlooking the Singapore River, it offers great accebility to nearby stores and restaurants.

The OrchardGate Hotel

The OrchardGate Hotel is on the rooftop of the OrchardGate Mall. It's a very affordable and relatively new hotel. You can book with a complementary breakfast and the nearby mall allows for multiple shops and restaurants. The area also has great access to public transportation and allows you to be in the center of Singapore.
